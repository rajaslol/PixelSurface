FAQ

Q: What is Pixel Surface?
A: Pixel Surface is a minimalist online tool that converts your images into pixel-art style with adjustable filters.

Q: Does it work on mobile?
A: Yes, the design is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.

Q: Do I need to install anything?
A: No installation required — everything runs directly in your browser.

Q: Can I save my edited images?
A: Yes, use the “⬇ Download” button to save your pixel-styled image as PNG.

Q: Are my images stored online?
A: No, all processing happens locally in your browser. We do not upload or store your files.

About Us

Pixel Surface was built for creators, developers, and pixel-art lovers who want a clean, easy tool to transform their images into retro-inspired styles. Our mission is to keep things fast, free, and user-friendly.
